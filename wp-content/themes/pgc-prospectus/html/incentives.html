Incentives

INCENTIVES AND SCHOLARSHIPS

At Punjab Colleges, we believe that hard working students deserve rewards, and that every child deserves an education. Incentives and scholarships have always been a source of encouragement and much needed support for students.

MERIT BASED SCHOLARSHIP

Punjab Group of Colleges promotes hard work and excellence. We offer an exclusive scholarship to students who show excellent performance in academics, they can avail up to 100% Fee Waiver. Students with 75% or above marks are eligible to apply for free education at the time of admission. Whereas, students with 70% or above marks can apply for a special fee concession of 50% percent.

TEACHER’S CHILDREN

Punjab Colleges facilitate the teachers and ensures that their children can avail high quality education. Children of teachers at public sector and known private Institutes are eligible for this concession.

1122 EMPLOYEES

The employees and paramedical staff of Rescue 1122 risk their lives in the line of duty. Punjab Colleges is providing special concession for the children of Rescue 1122 employees.

SHUHDA’S CHILDREN

Punjab Colleges take special care of children of Shuhada of armed forces and police of our country who sacrificed their lives for us. We provide a specific concession to Shuhda’s children.

STUDENTS WITH SPECIAL NEEDS

To motivate students with special needs for getting education and illuminating their minds with spark of hope, a special fee concession opportunity has been ensured for them.

NEED BASE CONCESSION

Knowing the fact that not all students can afford the fee and face economic crisis, PGC provides financial support by giving them concessions or free education.

ORPHAN STUDENTS

Orphans are entitled to a special scholarship at Punjab Colleges. Those who have lost their fathers/guardians, they can apply under this category.

KINSHIP

Punjab Colleges take care of the family of its students. The direct family members of old & current students are offered a special discount for their education.

SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES FINANCIAL AID

If the father/guardian of any current student unfortunately passes away, the responsibility of their fee will be taken over by Punjab Colleges itself, and education will be free for the student.

INCENTIVE POLICIES FOR MATRIC TOPPERS

Students securing top positions in matric from all boards are awarded with a brand new car/cash prizes upon joining Punjab Colleges.

INCENTIVE POLICY FOR PGC STUDENT IN UNIVERSITIES

Old students of PGC can avail concession at the time of admission at University of Central Punjab-Lahore, Mohammad Ali Jinnah University-Karachi, and Capital University of Science & Technology-Islamabad.
