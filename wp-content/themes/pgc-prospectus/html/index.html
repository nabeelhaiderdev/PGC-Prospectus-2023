About
PUNJAB COLLEGES

Started in 1985 in Lahore, the college has expanded its network across Punjab. With 438+ campuses in 130+ cities, Punjab Colleges has always taken pride in the quality of education it delivers. Over the years, our students have held top positions in Pakistan’s national board examinations for intermediate. Not only this, our students left a mark in the field of sports, winning general trophies consistently.

INCENTIVES

Position holders are the pride of their alma mater. Punjab Colleges offer a number of incentives for all those students who perform exceptionally well in their Board/ University exams. Other than incentives on the basis of performance, Punjab Group of Colleges is also concerned about the need of education of those students who can't afford the fee and provide financial support by giving them concession or free education through different categories.
