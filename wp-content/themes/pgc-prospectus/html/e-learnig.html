MICROSOFT CERTIFICATION

Punjab Group of Colleges in collaboration with Microsoft is offering free Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS) Certification to its students. The Microsoft Office Specialist Certification is a cost-effective, market-oriented solution to validate fundamental productivity, knowledge, and skills in demand today. We offer free certification in three categories which are MS Word, MS PowerPoint and MS Excel. Through this facility, students can save up to $160. Any student can avail this facility without having to pay extra fee.

VIRTUAL PROGRAMMES

The e-learning system of education is now an intrinsic part of the teaching and learning process. To ensure the availability of access to education, Punjab Colleges offer a state-of-the-art e-learning portal. This online system includes interactive video lectures and other electronic resources beneficial for the students. To guide our students in a better way, we are also offering virtual classrooms that connect students to their teachers in real-time so that they can communicate in a classroom environment.
