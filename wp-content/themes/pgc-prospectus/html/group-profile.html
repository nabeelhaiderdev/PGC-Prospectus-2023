ABOUT THE GROUP

Over the past 36 years, Punjab Group has expanded from a single college in Lahore to a massive cross-industry network spanning Pakistan. The success of this group is a testament to the sheer hard work and commitment to serve the people of Pakistan.

Today, Punjab Group is the leader in academic excellence, mass media communication and many other business ventures in Pakistan including urban development, renewable energy solutions, health, technology and agri-based products.

Zero Carbon

Zero Carbon is Pakistan's first renewable energy solution dedicated to combat climate change through the provision of clean, reliable and resilient energy solutions. The aim is to accelerate the transition of the country into a low Carbon & energy independent state, with a much greener tomorrow for generations to come. Zero Carbon holds the highest category license, C-1, issued by the Alternative Energy Development Board (AEDB).
80 MWs Installed in last 3 year, 105,242 tonnes Co2 Reduction, 193,330,116 kWh Annual Units

Fiska Tech

Established in the year 2019, FiskaTech is the Largest Setup of Biofloc Fish Farming in Pakistan. With the effective use of Biofloc Technology, FiskaTech makes it possible to minimize water exchange & water usage in aquaculture systems through maintaining adequate water quality within the culture unit while producing low cost bioflocs rich in protein, which in turn can serve as a feed for aquatic organisms. FiskaTech is steadfast in its mission to constitute a sustainable aquaculture and aims to make sustainable fish farming normative & a long term solution to the ever increasing food needs of growing population.

800,000+ People Served, 4.5 Billion+ PKR Distributed
