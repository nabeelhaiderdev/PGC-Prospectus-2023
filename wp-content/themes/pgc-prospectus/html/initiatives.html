Initiatives

Initiatives

HEALTH & SAFETY MEASURES

Punjab Colleges are committed to providing a safe, healthy, and environmentally responsible learning experience for its students. We believe that no task or activity is so important that it can’t be done in a safe manner and in compliance with all applicable safety standards. In all campuses, health and safety plans are intact to prevent any unfortunate event such as a fire or a medical emergency.

SOLAR ENERGY

To build a greener tomorrow for generations to come, Punjab Group of Colleges opted for the most sustainable form of energy by installing solar power plants at all major campuses.

MAKE PAKISTAN GREEN

To nip the menace of pollution and SMOGS in the bud, Punjab Group of Colleges has started a great initiative “Make Pakistan Green” of planting 2.5 million trees. In this campaign 5 lac students will be the agents of change to mitigate the decreasing quality of air and to evoke a sense of conserving nature.

ROAD SAFETY

To promote safe driving behavior and to invoke a sense of responsibility among the students, Punjab Group of Colleges initiated a campaign called “Helmet Saves Life”. In this campaign, Punjab Group of Colleges distributed free helmets throughout the campuses.

ANTI-DRUGS DRIVE

TTo eradicate the menace of drugs from Pakistan, there is dire need to create an awareness about the harmful effects of drugs. In this regard PGC launched a corporate social responsibility campaign “Drug Free Pakistan” and shed light on the repercussions of drug abuse.

CLEAN DRINKING WATER

Punjab Group of Colleges is not merely striving to provide quality education but also deeply concerned about the health of its students and masses. PGC took a great initiative “Drink Clean with PGC”.

RIDE WITH PRIDE

In 2019, Punjab Group of Colleges conducted a research on the commute issues faced by the 51 percent population of country i.e. females. Under the light of the research, an initiative with the name “Ride with Pride” was announced.

KASUR MUWAKHAT PROGRAMME

At Punjab Group of Colleges, giving back to the community has always been our core philosophy. To fulfill this purpose, Dunya Foundation started a project in 2014 by the name of Kasur Muwakhat programme (KMP) with the aim to reduce poverty and increase capacity building.
